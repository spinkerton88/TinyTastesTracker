# Privacy Policy for Tiny Tastes Tracker

**Last Updated:** January 12, 2026

## Introduction

Welcome to Tiny Tastes Tracker ("we," "our," or "the App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and the privacy of your children. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect information when you use our baby tracking application.

**Important:** Tiny Tastes Tracker is designed to help parents track their baby's nutrition, sleep, growth, and development. We take the privacy and security of your family's data very seriously.

## Information We Collect

### Data You Provide Directly

When you use Tiny Tastes Tracker, you may provide the following information:

#### Child Profile Information
- Child's name, date of birth, and gender
- Birth weight, length, and head circumference
- Current age and developmental stage
- Allergen information and dietary restrictions
- Profile photos (optional)

#### Health & Activity Tracking
- **Meal Logs**: Foods eaten, portion sizes, feeding times, feeding strategies
- **Sleep Logs**: Sleep duration, wake times, sleep quality notes
- **Diaper Logs**: Diaper changes, types, and timestamps
- **Bottle Logs**: Bottle feeding times, amounts, and types
- **Growth Data**: Weight, height, and head circumference measurements over time
- **Developmental Milestones**: Notes and observations about your child's development

#### Recipes & Meal Planning
- Custom recipes you create or save
- Meal plans and shopping lists
- Photos of meals (optional)
- Recipe notes and modifications

#### AI Interactions
- Questions you ask Sage AI (our AI assistant)
- Voice chat recordings (processed in real-time, not stored)
- Photos you share for food recognition or analysis
- Feedback you provide on AI recommendations

### Data Collected Automatically

- **App Usage Data**: Features you use, screens you view, and how you interact with the app
- **Device Information**: Device type, iOS version, app version
- **Performance Data**: Crash reports, error logs, and performance metrics (if you opt-in)

### Data We Do NOT Collect

- We do **NOT** collect your email address, phone number, or other contact information
- We do **NOT** track your location
- We do **NOT** use cookies or tracking pixels
- We do **NOT** collect data for advertising purposes
- We do **NOT** sell your data to third parties

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to:

1. **Provide Core Functionality**
   - Track your child's meals, sleep, growth, and development
   - Generate personalized recommendations based on your child's age and history
   - Create meal plans and shopping lists
   - Display growth charts and nutritional analysis

2. **AI-Powered Features**
   - Answer your questions about baby nutrition and development
   - Analyze photos to identify foods and suggest serving methods
   - Generate age-appropriate meal suggestions
   - Provide personalized feeding strategies

3. **Improve the App**
   - Fix bugs and improve performance
   - Understand which features are most valuable
   - Develop new features based on usage patterns

4. **Ensure Safety**
   - Detect and prevent misuse of AI features
   - Monitor API usage to prevent abuse
   - Protect against security threats

## How We Store Your Information

### Local Storage (On Your Device)

**Most of your data is stored locally on your device** using Apple's SwiftData framework:

- All meal logs, sleep logs, diaper logs, and bottle logs
- Growth measurements and charts
- Child profiles and settings
- Recipes and meal plans
- Shopping lists
- Photos you upload

**This data:**
- Remains on your device
- Is protected by your device's passcode/biometric security
- Is included in your device's encrypted backups (iCloud or iTunes)
- Is **NOT** transmitted to our servers
- Is deleted when you delete the app (unless backed up)

### Cloud Processing (Third-Party Services)

Some features require sending data to third-party services:

#### Google Gemini AI
- **What is sent**: Your questions, meal descriptions, photos for analysis, child's age and allergen information (for context)
- **Purpose**: To provide AI-powered recommendations, answer questions, and analyze food photos
- **Data retention**: Google processes this data according to their [Gemini API Terms of Service](https://ai.google.dev/terms)
- **Privacy**: Google may use this data to improve their AI models. We recommend not including personally identifiable information in your AI queries.

#### Open Food Facts API
- **What is sent**: Barcode numbers when you scan packaged foods
- **Purpose**: To retrieve nutrition information for packaged foods
- **Data retention**: Open Food Facts is a non-profit open database. Barcode lookups are not linked to your identity.
- **Privacy**: [Open Food Facts Privacy Policy](https://world.openfoodfacts.org/privacy)

#### Apple EventKit/Reminders (Optional)
- **What is sent**: Shopping list items (if you choose to export to Reminders)
- **Purpose**: To sync your shopping list with the iOS Reminders app
- **Data retention**: Stored in your iCloud account according to Apple's privacy policy
- **Privacy**: You control this data through iOS Settings

- Parents provide information about their children

### Data About Children

## Your Rights and Choices

### Access Your Data
All your data is stored locally on your device. You can view it anytime within the app.

### Export Your Data (Data Portability)
You can export all your data in JSON or CSV format:
1. Go to **Settings** → **Privacy & Data**
2. Tap **Export All Data**
3. Choose your preferred format (JSON or CSV)
4. Share or save the file

This allows you to:
- Keep a backup of your data
- Transfer data to another app
- Exercise your GDPR "right to data portability"

### Delete Your Data
You can delete your data at any time:

**Delete Specific Data Types:**
1. Go to **Settings** → **Privacy & Data** → **Delete Data**
2. Choose what to delete (meal logs, sleep logs, growth data, etc.)
3. Confirm deletion

**Delete All Data:**
1. Go to **Settings** → **Privacy & Data** → **Delete All Data**
2. Confirm deletion (this cannot be undone)

**Delete the App:**
- Deleting the app from your device removes all locally stored data
- Data sent to third-party services (Google Gemini) may be retained according to their policies

### Opt-Out of AI Features
If you prefer not to use AI features that send data to Google:
1. Simply don't use the Sage AI chat feature
2. Don't use photo analysis features
3. The app's core tracking features work entirely offline

### Manage Permissions
You can control app permissions in iOS Settings:
- **Photos**: Required for uploading meal photos and recipe images
- **Camera**: Required for scanning barcodes and taking photos
- **Reminders**: Optional, only needed for shopping list export

## Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

### On-Device Security
- Data is stored using Apple's SwiftData framework with encryption
- Protected by your device's passcode/biometric authentication
- Encrypted backups via iCloud or iTunes

### API Security
- Google Gemini API key is obfuscated (not stored in plaintext)
- Rate limiting prevents API abuse (10 requests/min, 100/hour, 1000/day)
- API usage monitoring detects anomalies
- Secure HTTPS connections for all network requests

### Limitations
- No system is 100% secure
- We cannot guarantee absolute security
- You are responsible for keeping your device secure

## Data Retention

### Local Data
- Data is retained on your device until you delete it
- Deleting the app removes all local data (unless backed up)

### Third-Party Services
- **Google Gemini**: Retains data according to their terms (typically 30 days for API requests)
- **Open Food Facts**: Barcode lookups are not stored with your identity

### Backups
- If you use iCloud or iTunes backup, your data is included
- Restoring from a backup restores your Tiny Tastes Tracker data
- You can disable app data backup in iOS Settings

## International Users (GDPR Compliance)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have additional rights under GDPR:

### Legal Basis for Processing
We process your data based on:
- **Consent**: You provide data voluntarily to use the app
- **Legitimate Interest**: To provide and improve our services

### Your GDPR Rights
- **Right to Access**: View all your data in the app
- **Right to Rectification**: Edit your data anytime
- **Right to Erasure**: Delete your data anytime
- **Right to Data Portability**: Export your data in JSON/CSV format
- **Right to Restrict Processing**: Stop using AI features
- **Right to Object**: Contact us to object to data processing
- **Right to Withdraw Consent**: Delete the app or stop using AI features

### Data Transfers
- Data is stored locally on your device (in your country)
- AI features send data to Google servers (may be processed in the US)
- Google complies with EU-US Data Privacy Framework
