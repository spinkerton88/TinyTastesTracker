# Support

## Getting Help with Tiny Tastes Tracker

If you need help with the app or have questions:

### In-App Help
- Open the app and tap **Settings** → **Help & Support**
- Use the **Sage AI** assistant for quick answers about baby nutrition and development

### Contact Us

**For General Support:**
- Email: support@tinytastestracker.com
- Response time: We aim to respond within 2-3 business days

**For Privacy or Data Concerns:**
- Email: privacy@tinytastestracker.com
- See our [Privacy Policy](/) for more information

**For Technical Issues:**
- Email: support@tinytastestracker.com
- Include: iOS version, app version, and description of the issue

### Common Questions

**How do I export my data?**
Go to Settings → Privacy & Data → Export All Data

**How do I delete my data?**
Go to Settings → Privacy & Data → Delete Data

**How do I sync across devices?**
Enable iCloud sync in Settings → CloudKit Sync

**The AI isn't working. What should I do?**
1. Check your internet connection
2. Make sure you're running the latest version of the app
3. Contact support if the issue persists

### Report a Bug
Found a bug? Please email support@tinytastestracker.com with:
- Description of the issue
- Steps to reproduce
- Screenshots (if applicable)
- Your device model and iOS version
